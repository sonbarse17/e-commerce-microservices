Add this dependency to all service pom.xml files:

<dependency>
    <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
    <artifactId>spring-boot-starter-actuator</artifactId>
</dependency>

Add this to all service application.yml files:

management:
  endpoints:
    web:
      exposure:
        include: health,info
  endpoint:
    health:
      show-details: always
